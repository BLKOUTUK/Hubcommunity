
BLKOUTNXT Signup Form Embed Instructions
=========================================

To embed the BLKOUTNXT signup form on your website, follow these steps:

1. Copy the HTML code below
2. Paste it into your website's HTML where you want the form to appear
3. Replace "https://your-app-url.com" with your actual deployed application URL

Embed Code:
-----------

<iframe 
  src="https://your-app-url.com/embed/signup-form.html" 
  width="100%" 
  height="650" 
  frameborder="0"
  style="max-width: 450px; margin: 0 auto; display: block;"
  title="BLKOUTNXT Signup Form">
</iframe>

Customization:
-------------
- You can adjust the width and height parameters to fit your website's design
- The max-width style ensures the form doesn't get too wide on large screens
- For responsive designs, keep width="100%" and adjust max-width as needed
